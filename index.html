What we optimize for (Schengen / ICAO)

The app guides composition toward the ICAO Doc 9303 standard most Schengen countries reference. Always confirm the latest rules from your consulate, VFS Global, TLScontact, or BLS International portal.

  • 35×45 mm canvas — exported at 420×540 px, the standard passport-photo proportion used across the Schengen area.
  • Face height 70–80% — matches the ICAO head-size guidance (crown to chin), shown with a live percentage readout.
  • Per-country background — white by default; automatically light gray for Switzerland, and a white/gray toggle for Germany and France.
  • JPEG size policy — export compressed to ≤512 KB, sized for VFS Global / TLScontact / BLS International online upload portals.
  • Submission notes — each country shows which visa service provider it routes through and a link to the official guidance.
  • No face reshaping — segmentation and cropping only; we don't slim or resize facial geometry.
  • Privacy — segmentation and matting run on your iPhone; no separate upload to our servers for processing.

Final acceptance is always at the discretion of each Schengen country's consulate, embassy, or visa service provider.
